Job Description

The Albert B. Alkek Library at Texas State University is seeking qualified forward-thinking, user-oriented, energetic, collaborative applicants for the position of Computer Science/Computer Information Systems (CIS)/Mathematics Librarian. Reporting to the Head of Research, Instruction & Outreach, this position will focus on intensive outreach to the Texas State campus community, resulting in rich engagement with assigned subject disciplines, with particular emphasis on outreach to faculty, graduate students, and upper division undergraduate students. Methods of interaction with disciplines will vary and could include, but are not limited to: research assistance, instruction, and collection development. This position will serve as the subject specialist in Computer Science, Mathematics, and CIS and Qualitative Studies.
Job Duties

Provide in-depth, specialized research assistance in a variety of ways and locations. Teach formal library instruction sessions in both traditional and electronic classroom settings. Participate in evaluation of the instruction program and outcome assessment of instruction sessions. Promote use of subject-specific information resources and services to meet user needs and expectations, utilizing current technologies and information tools. Serve as primary liaison and information specialist for subject areas. Assess user needs and analyze trends in assigned disciplines or subject areas. Develop and manage collections in assigned subject areas and the reference collection. Manage collection funds effectively and in a timely manner. Maintain awareness of department or college’s current and future programs. Initiate ongoing communication regarding faculty and student resource needs and manage service expectations. Assist faculty with data management and provide resources and training as needed. Work with relevant library staff and/or IT Division staff in order to assist faculty. Serve in interim roles including on standing and ad hoc teams and task forces and assist in development and management of special projects as needed. Maintain a professional demeanor and work collaboratively as a team member. Engage in professional activities in accordance with Texas State University’s standards for promotion for professional librarians.
