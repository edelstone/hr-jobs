Welcome to the most beautiful campus in Texas.

Our surroundings make the Texas State experience truly unique. A quick stroll across our San Marcos campus will lead you past some of the state's greatest treasures:

  • The San Marcos River bubbles up from the Edwards Aquifer right on our grounds.
  • Old Main, our first building, is the centerpiece of a varied and distinct architecture.
  • State champion trees line the walkways on our official Arbor Day Foundation-designated Tree Campus USA.

Though our campus is stunning, what really makes Texas State unique is the people who work here. Prepare yourself to work with some of the most dedicated and passionate folks around.
